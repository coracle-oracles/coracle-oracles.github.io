Coracle 2023

Coracle is happening this year from November 3-5 at Shakori Hills in Pittsboro NC.

Guided by the 10 principles of Burning Man, Coracle celebrates art, community, play and expression. Children of all ages are welcome.

The venue is the grounds of Shakori Hills Community Arts Center in Pittsboro, NC! Plenty of room for an intimate gathering.
