Coracle Regional Burn is in Pittsboro, NC on Nov. 2-3rd, 2019, with early access on Nov. 1st for theme camp and art build.

Guided by the 10 principles of Burning Man, Coracle celebrates art, community, play and expression. Children of all ages are welcome.

The venue is the grounds of Shakori Hills Community Arts Center in Pittsboro, NC! Plenty of room for an intimate gathering. Tickets are limited to 125 folks.

Future Reflections

The 2019 Coracle Art Theme is ‘Future Reflections.’ We invite you to find your own meaning in this theme and allow it to guide art and participation for Coracle in November.

Future reflections can show themselves in many forms. Glimpses of light found throughout our lives. Your present self, seen through the eyes of your malleable future ghost.

A swift review of our founding feats. The good, the bad and the necessary we face.

In order to look on our future selves while in the present, we must first muster an image of not only who, but what we will be. Who are you in the future mirror of your mind? Once you find that image, reflect on how you got there.

Future reflection is an idea, a question and a challenge to look within. A pause, a breath. Who were you? Who are you and who will you become?

Future reflections are the ghosts of both what has and has not yet come. A pathway through time and consciousness, a route yet to be set that may be changed as we walk backwards along it to where we are, where we once were and where we will be.

With so many ways to think about future reflections, we invite you to find your own and share it with the community this year at Coracle through art, music, conversation, experience or any way you feel fit. See you in the very near future!
